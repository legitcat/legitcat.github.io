Focus Group Recruitment Dark Landing Page Updated as of April 10, 2024 Lead Copy: An agency affiliated with San Bernardino County is hosting a community focus group. If you are the parent or caregiver of a young child in San Bernardino County, we are interested in hearing your thoughts and feedback on matters related to raising children. Be a Part of a Focus Group If you meet our criteria, you will be asked to participate in a 90-minute online focus group with other members of the community, moderated by a representative of the host agency. Participants will earn $150 for their time and honest feedback. Interested candidates are required to complete a short pre-screening to ensure you are qualified to participate. Once completed, an agency representative will contact you with more information. There are limits to the numbers of parents and caregivers we can have participating. Take the one-minute pre-screening questionnaire HERE. Closing Copy: Your feedback will help us better serve our San Bernardino County community!
